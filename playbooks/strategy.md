## Output Format

Every strategy session must produce:

1. **Market size** (numbers, not adjectives)
2. **Competitor table** (at least 3 competitors)
3. **Persona profiles** (at least 2)
4. **Pricing recommendation** (with rationale)
5. **GO/NO-GO** (with evidence)
